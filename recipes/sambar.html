Sambar

Description

Sambar (English: /sʌmbʌʀ/, romanized: Sāmbār) is a lentil-based vegetable stew/porridge/soup, cooked with pigeon pea and tamarind broth. It is popular in South Indian and Sri Lankan cuisines.

According to food historian K. T. Achaya, the earliest extant mention of sambar in literature can be dated to the 17th century.[1]
